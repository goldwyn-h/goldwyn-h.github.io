Every parents wants the best for the their children, so much that we overwhelm them with weekend activities, without realising that is unhealthy for them. The purpose of education is to empower our children, not work them to the bones. Scoll down below to find out how You can maximise your children's potential without burning them out.

Knowing yourself

Knowing our children's strengths and weaknesses. There are many sports faciliaties in Singapore, basketball/soccer etc.These sports require interactions with objects. For them to excel in sports, we often neglect the need to fine-tune the fundamental; co-ordination; running / jumping /rolling. This can be avoided by training their motor skills. By developing their motor skills in the early days of their childhood, Your child have the advantage of knowing their area of improvement, all you need is to train them in those aspects. Once they are good, they move on to improving the skills aspect of the sports that they are interested in/good at.
